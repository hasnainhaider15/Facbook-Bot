						Facebook Bot


Created a facebook automated bot to control user actions automatically.

The main purpose of this bot is to send random generated messages including the same profile name for business 
purposes to people in groups or individuals.

Bot logs into facebook and store the session for next time use.

It has two options.

	1. end messages to group members. and send friend request also. Dynamic choice to insert group name, 
	group limit, people limit in group members section and group location.

	2. Send message to Individual person and send friend requests also. Dynamic choice to insert person 
	name, and limit of people to send messages and friend requests.

Tools: Python and selenium
